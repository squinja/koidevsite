Artificia
A cyberpunk, near-future dystopian world. A powerful government that controls all means of production. Robots that back every aspect of human life, now turning against their owners in seemingly random occurrences.

You are Luna Ashfall--pink-haired, industrious daughter of the lead engineer of bots, Orvis Ashfall. Your father was just arrested for suspicion of treason for implanting a bug within robots that allowed them to rebel. How will you save your father and prevent a disaster from happening between the robots and your government?
